For the pet that hates the salon, the van changes everything

Most dogs and cats do not misbehave because they are difficult. They react to the car ride, the waiting room, the crate, and a room full of barking dogs.

Professional Pet Grooming takes all of that away. A self-contained grooming van pulls up to your home, and your pet is groomed one on one with you close by. It is calmer for nervous, senior, and small pets, and far simpler for you.

  • Your pet stays home, in a place it already knows and trusts.
  • One pet at a time. No kennel of waiting animals, ever.
  • No drop-off or pickup. We arrive, we groom, you keep your day.
  • You can watch the whole visit. Nothing is hidden.
